#### Project Title
##### Sales Data Analysis – Identifying Opportunities to Increase Sales

#### Project Overview
This project analyzes a sales transaction dataset to identify patterns that may help increase overall sales performance. The analysis focuses on product performance, sales trends over time, regional sales distribution, and potential product bundling opportunities.

#### Dataset
Source: Kaggle
Practice EDA on this Sales Dataset – Dhruv Kothari
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/dhruvkothari19/practice-eda-on-this-sales-dataset

#### Project Objectives
- Identify top-performing products by revenue
- Analyze monthly and seasonal sales trends
- Identify top-performing regions
- Explore product combinations for potential bundling
